## Collection Methodology

Data compiled from the Kenya Fisheries Service Catch Assessment Surveys (CAS) conducted at marine landing sites. Fisheries enumerators record species composition, landed catch weight, fishing effort, and market value through regular sampling at landing sites, aggregated annually by county.

## SEEA EA Relevance

- **Provisioning services:** Direct input for wild fish provisioning service account (catch volume + ex-vessel value); used for economic valuation
- **Baseline:** 2020-2024 time series enables trend analysis for accounting period (2025-2026)
- **Cross-reference:** Group-level data can be linked to family-level fish UVC data from coral reef surveys
- **Data gaps:**
  - County-level aggregate only; not disaggregated by ecosystem type (coral reef vs. seagrass vs. mangrove)
  - No fishing effort data (vessel days, gear type)
  - Values in KES — require USD conversion for SEEA EA standard accounts
  - Unreported and subsistence landings not captured

## Data Quality

- **Validated:** Yes — extracted from officially published KeFS Bulletins and cross-checked with original tables
- **Fitness for purpose:** High — suitable for fisheries production trend analysis, species composition, economic valuation, SEEA ocean accounting
- **Known issues:**

  - 2023 catch and value totals are estimates
