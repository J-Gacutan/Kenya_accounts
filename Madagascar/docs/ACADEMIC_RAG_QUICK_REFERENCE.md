## Citation Confidence Levels

### HIGH CONFIDENCE (Use in policy briefs)
- ✅ Peer-reviewed sources (all 6, especially 2020+)
- ✅ UN/Government official documents
- ✅ Multi-author studies with institutional backing (Rwanda, SA)

### MEDIUM CONFIDENCE (Use with context)
- ⚠️ Gray literature from credible institutions (WAVES, UNSD)
- ⚠️ Preliminary reports marked as "experimental"
- ⚠️ Case studies without full peer review

### LOWER CONFIDENCE (Flag if citing)
- ⚠️ Single-author gray literature
- ⚠️ Preliminary or paywalled sources
- ⚠️ Sources with uncertain access
