Biomass Calculation
4

Fish Biomass: Allometric Conversion W = a × Lb

For each fish record, calculate individual weight from size-class midpoint and species-specific allometric parameters (124 unique a, 55 unique b values).

Size midpoints: 5-10=7.5, 10-20=15, 20-30=25, 30-40=35, 40-50=45, 50-60=55 cm W_grams = a × L^b × count biomass_kg_ha = (sum(W_grams) / 1000) / TRANSECT_AREA_HA

CRITICAL: TRANSECT_AREA = 100 m2 assumed (20×5 m). If 400 m2 (20×20 m), all values ÷ 4. PENDING
