Why soil carbon matters: Mangrove soils can hold 50-60% of total ecosystem carbon in belowground stocks -- in some systems up to 90%. Field soil coring surveys planned for 2026 will quantify this component. VM0033 permits use of default soil carbon values (1.46 tC/ha/yr) as an interim measure, meaning credit issuance can begin before field soil data is complete.
Assumptions and caveats: Revenue estimates apply typical intact-forest sequestration rates (4-5 tC/ha/yr) to forests currently operating at one-tenth of structural potential (CCI 0.07-0.12). Actual sequestration under current degraded conditions is likely lower; figures represent potential under restored conditions. Voluntary carbon market rates of USD 5-10/tC used. Horizon 3 estimate applies a 1.5-1.6x multiplier to account for belowground carbon. All figures are indicative, not contracted revenue projections. VM0033-registered mangrove restoration credits typically command a premium over standard avoidance credits.
