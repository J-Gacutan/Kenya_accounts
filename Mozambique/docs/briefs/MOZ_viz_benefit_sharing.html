Carbon Credit Revenue: Benefit-Sharing Waterfall

How gross credit revenue is distributed under Mozambique's legal framework and the MozBlue precedent
Decree 81/2023 -- Legal floor Minimum 20% of gross credit revenue to communities. Minimum 15% to the state. A Benefit-Sharing Agreement (BSA) is a legal prerequisite for carbon credit validation. DUAT and customary occupation rights form the foundation on which the BSA is built.
MozBlue precedent -- Operational benchmark 33.3% to communities via community fishing councils, exceeding the legal minimum. 7,500+ direct beneficiaries, 50% women. This tripartite model (developer / government / communities) has become the standard for blue carbon in Mozambique.
