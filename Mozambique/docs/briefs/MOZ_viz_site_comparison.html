Heterogeneous mosaic -- selective clearing with refugia Two productive patches (Assets 3 and 8) show biomass of 21-27 Mg/ha and canopy closure above 30%. The remaining six assets are sparse woodlands at 9-15 Mg/ha. This pattern suggests past selective clearing with mature forest pockets remaining. Restoration potential is not foreclosed.
Uniform systemic stress -- possible freshwater deficit All three assets are remarkably similar: 5.2-5.8 m height, 10.2-11.3 Mg/ha biomass, 4.6-5.1% cover. Uniform stress across the entire site points to a systemic driver. Satellite data are consistent with freshwater deficit; upstream river regulation is one plausible cause requiring hydrological investigation.
