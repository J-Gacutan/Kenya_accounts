Mangrove Extent

Kilifi County Mangrove Extent Account

Mangrove extent along the Kilifi coastline was assessed using satellite-derived mapping (GMW 2020 baseline vs 2025 project classification). Despite 66% of mangrove falling within protected areas, extent declined by 5.6% over 5 years. See Mangrove Condition for field-based condition survey results.

Extent Account (2020-2025)

The mangrove extent account tracks change between the Global Mangrove Watch (GMW) v4 2020 baseline (Sentinel-2 analysis at 10 m spatial resolution) and the 2025 project classification.
